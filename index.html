Professor Dr von Igelfeld

I am a Professor at the Institute of Romance Philology, University of Regensburg, Germany. My research focuses on Portuguese irregular verbs. I studied under Professor Dr Dieter Vogelsang in Munich and later worked as the second assistant of Professor Walter Schoeffler-Henschell at the University of Wiesbaden.
